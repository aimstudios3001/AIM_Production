A B O U T

G+ Studio is the creative heartbeat of Aim Production, a multidisciplinary design and media powerhouse committed to turning imagination into powerful visual experiences. Born from a passion for art, design, and storytelling, G+ Studio brings together a team of artists, animators, editors, designers, and motion experts dedicated to crafting unique and compelling content for brands, creators, and innovators across the globe.

We don’t just make visuals — we design experiences. Whether you're launching a product, telling your brand story, creating social media content, producing cinematic visuals, or designing a stunning identity — G+ Studio is your all-in-one creative engine.

Graphic Design

From minimalist logos to complex brand systems, social media graphics, print materials, merchandise, and corporate identity packages — our designs are not just eye-catching; they're intentional. Every pixel we craft is driven by strategy, meaning, and aesthetic harmony.

Motion Graphics & Video Production

We transform stories into dynamic, moving visuals that engage, inform, and entertain. With expert video editing, seamless transitions, kinetic typography, and animated infographics, our motion design team breathes life into your message — whether it’s a commercial, explainer video, YouTube intro, or cinematic short.

2D/3D Animation

From frame-by-frame hand-drawn styles to complex 3D rigged character animations, we create stunning animated content that fits any tone — playful, educational, professional, or emotional. Our animations have been used in games, music videos, branding campaigns, training content, and beyond.

VFX / SFX

Whether you need epic explosions, futuristic HUDs, natural elements like fire or water, or seamless green screen compositing, our visual effects (VFX) and sound effects (SFX) team will add the magic your production needs. We also specialize in film-grade post-production techniques.

Audio Design & Sound Editing

Visuals without sound are incomplete. Our audio specialists handle everything from voice-over mixing to original background scores and immersive sound effects that pull your audience deeper into your story.

Branding & Identity

More than just logos, we build brands — visual identities with personality, purpose, and consistency. From color palettes to typography systems, brand guidelines to packaging design, we help you craft a voice and look that lasts.
